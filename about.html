ANNA URIK

... is a photographer and filmmaker based in London.


Originally from Prague, she is based in London but she is usually on the move (for pleasure or work).


Her work encompasses all sorts of nuances, ranging from polished, eclectic fashion shoots to gritty anthropological documentaries.


She is strong opinioned, never compromising her vision - but she keeps this from ruining all the fun.


She can adjust to the needs of different projects and yet both her personal and commissioned projects are somehow always defined by selfsarcasm, humor and a subtle unconventionality that goes beyond any hip rules.
