ANNA URIK


... is a photographer and filmmaker.


Originally from Prague, she is based in London but is usually on the move (for work or leisure).


Her work encompasses all sorts of nuances: ranging from polished, eclectic fashion shoots to anthropological documentaries.


She has strong opinions and never compromises her vision - while never ruining the funny side of things.


She can adjust to the needs of different projects and yet both her personal and commissioned projects are somehow always defined by authenticiy, humour and a nuanced unconventionality that goes beyond the hip rules.
